Vision
Overview

Renesas’ Vision AI delivers high‑performance, low‑power computer vision through its DRP‑AI accelerator of MPUs and scalable lineup of MCUs, enabling efficient processing for applications ranging from building automations, industrial inspection, and to robotics. The extensive library of pre‑trained models and developer‑friendly workflows allows all skill levels to rapidly prototype and deploy vision solutions at the edge​.
