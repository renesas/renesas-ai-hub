Overview

Renesas’ Real-Time Analytics brings advanced signal processing, machine learning, and anomaly detection directly to MCUs and MPUs for fast, accurate edge intelligence. It enables predictive maintenance, equipment monitoring, and user and environmental sensing with a compact, explainable, and fully integrated toolchain.
